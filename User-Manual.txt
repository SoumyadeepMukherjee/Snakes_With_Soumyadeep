## General Instructions :-

* Press Enter key to Start the game.

* Use the Arrow keys to move the Snake so as the snake eats the food and your score increments by 10.

* However if the Snake hits the boundaries or it collapses within against itself , then the Game ends.

* As the Game ends , you are given the option to quit the Game or continue playing the game by again pressing the Enter key.



## Cheatcodes of the Game :-

The user can press the Q key to fetch extra 10 points :)

Hopefully you have understood the instructions nicely. Now enjoy the Game :)
